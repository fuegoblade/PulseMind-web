### 🚀 System Architecture (Core Features)

PulseMind™ operates on the basis of dynamic evaluation. We do not recognize static tables. Here, live code rules:

* 🧬 **Digital Biological Avatar:** A unique data model that constantly learns from your behavior, stress, and biometric inputs (Garmin, Oura, Apple Health).
* 🧠 **Cognitive Core:** Advanced AI analysis that deciphers the connections between your fatigue, sleep architecture, and the central nervous system.
* 🥩 **Metabolic Architecture:** Exact nutritional protocols. Just take a photo of your food, and the AI instantly breaks down macronutrients and places them in the context of your energy expenditure.
* ⚡ **Adaptive Recalibration:** Performance analysis for elite athletes, intelligent return to movement after injury, and managed recovery of the nervous system.
* 🛡️ **Longevity & Growth:** Specialized modules for active longevity (Seniors), safe development (Children), and extreme metabolic transformations (Pregnancy).
